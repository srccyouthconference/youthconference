Welcome to 5th SRCC Youth Conference 2016!

Youth Conference is back!

Youth Conference is a student-to-student initiative whose objective is to stimulate the biggest (and currently latent) strength of an emerging nation, i.e. its youth to be held on September 20th and 21st this year.

It aims to bring together distinguished people, who have carved a niche in their respective fields, to share their milestones, downfalls, struggles and successes, while keeping in mind the conference theme which one of prevailing importance at large

It is a gateway into the world of the mavericks, who let the voice of their passion overpower the screams of naysayers; the resilient, who despite failures and pain kept moving forward; and finally the dreamers who worked their way into what has become our idea of success now. With journalists, corporates and social activists on one side to musicians, stand-up artists and actors on the other, Youth Conference creates two days of knowledge, learning, fun, music, experiences, laughter and inspiration all at the same time.

Registration will start soon! So mark your calendars and don't forget to register!!

We'll be posting more info here as we get closer to the event. In the mean time, follow us on our Youth Conference Facebook page for updates.
