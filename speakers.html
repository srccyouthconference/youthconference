SRCC Youth Conference 2016

September 20-21, Delhi

Speakers

Abhinav Bindra

@Abhinav_Bindra

Abhinav Bindra revolutionized the way sport was viewed in India. Born in a Punjabi family, his professional career took off in 2002 with his first medal in the Commonwealth Games. A spree of medals in the Commonwealth Games, Asian Games and the World Championships, his watershed moment, though came in 2008 when he became the first Indian to win an individual gold medal at the Olympics. A Padma Bhushan awardee, a Colorado University graduate and a successful businessman, Abhinav Bindra is among the best our country has ever produced. We at SRCC are proud to present before you Mr. Abhinav Bindra at the Youth Conference 2016.

Kailash Kher

Singer

@Kailashkher

Visit kailashkher.com

This Music Maestro dons many hats- a singer, a music-composer, a lyricist and a performer. Whether it be "Allah Ke Bande" or "Teri Deewani", he manages to strike a chord with the audience everytime he steps on stage. His modern fusion band- Kailasa, an amalgamation of Eastern and Western cultures, has pushed the envelope for innovation in the Music Industry. The idol of many and the inspiration to millions. We at SRCC are proud to have Mr. Kailash Kher at the Youth Conference 2016.

Soha Ali Khan

Actress

@sakpataudi

Our next speaker is a born genius, a versatile actress, daughter of India's first test captain and one of the most successful Indian actresses, and an alumni of prestigious institutions like Oxford University and London School of Economics. Her Bollywood career boasts successes like the super hit movie - Rang de Basanti. She has not only excelled in Bollywood but also has a successful career in the corporate world. We at SRCC are proud to present before you Ms. Soha Ali Khan as our next speaker at the Youth Conference 2016.

Piyush Pandey

Fondly referred to as “The Big Daddy of Indian Advertising”, this marketing guru is responsible for the most successful ad campaigns in the country. Having designed innumerable advertisements as the Co-Executive Chairman and National Creative Director of Ogilvy & Mather, he switched to a different genre. The result- the game-changing “Abki Baar Modi Sarkar”. A former Ranji player, a professional tea taster and more recently, a Padma Shri awardee, this man is creativity personified. We at SRCC are proud to have Mr. Piyush Pandey at the Youth Conference 2016.

Shashi Tharoor

Politician

@ShashiTharoor

Eloquence and charm best describe this person. A politician by day and an author by night, this man is a favourite among the youth. The most followed politician on Twitter until 2013, he has previously served as a United Nations Under Secretary General. A former Minister of State for HRD and External Affairs, Dr. Shashi Tharoor is a youth icon in the truest sense of the term. We at SRCC are proud to have Dr. Shashi Tharoor at the Youth Conference 2016.

Rahul Ram

Facebook

This man despite being an IIT graduate, vowed in his twenties to never wear a suit and do a 9-5 job. His unconventional career choices have taken him places- From being an activist for the Narmada Bachao Andolan to playing bass guitar for the Indian Ocean for the last 25 years and more recently venturing into the genre of political satire. Fondly referred to as 'Logic Baba' by his bandmates for his rational outlook, Rahul Ram signifies everything that the youth stand for. His courage, creativity and confidence make him a role model for the youth. We at SRCC are proud to have Mr.Rahul Ram at the Youth Conference 2016.

Anuja Chauhan

@anujachauhan

A woman of many words, this person wins over as many hearts with her charm as she does with her works. Her novels like 'The Zoya Factor' and 'Those Pricey Thakur Girls' have carved a niche for themselves. With a prior career in Advertising she has Campaigns such as Pepsi's 'Yeh Dil Maange More' to her credit. Voted as one of the most influential women in the country by MSN, Anuja Chauhan is an inspiration to young women all around the country. We at SRCC are proud to have Ms. Anuja Chauhan at the Youth Conference 2016.

Kishore Biyani

@kishore_biyani

This man's credentials speak for themselves. A corporate genius of the highest order, he changed the way retail shopping takes place in India. His retail chains like Big Bazaar, Central, Easy Day, Home Town , eZone, and Aadhaar are present in around 240 cities across India and attract over 370 million customers annually. His corporate credo, ‘Rewrite Rules, Retain Values,’ has inspired many young entrepreneurs in India. The founder and Group CEO of Future Group, Kishore Biyani is a thought leader in Indian business. We at SRCC are proud to have Mr. Kishore Biyani at the Youth Conference 2016.

Rocky and Mayur

@rockyandmayur

http://rockyandmayur.in

This next speaker duo believes that anything worth doing must be fun and unconventional.Widely acknowledged as India’s leading experts on street food and regional cuisine their cult show Highway On My Plate (HOMP) has aired on NDTV Goodtimes for over 7 years with over 230 episodes. Their passion for food and entertainment have made them a favourite in our generation.The next stop in their journey - SRCC Youth Conference. We at SRCC are proud to have Rocky and Mayur at the Youth Conference 2016.

Manish Paul

@ManishPaul03

Born and brought up in Delhi, this Television Icon is a household name. He holds the distinction of hosting the Mumbai Police Show for the 10 years and Jhalak Dikhla Ja for 5 years. Well-known for his style and sense of fashion, he has been declared as the Most Stylish Host by HT for the last 3 years. With critically acclaimed movies such as Tere Bin Laden and Micky Virus, he has added yet another feather to his hat. Manish Paul signifies everything that the Youth stands for. We at SRCC are proud to have Mr. Manish Paul at the Youth Conference 2016.

Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore

@Ra_THORe

This man has the distinction of serving India in diverse fields. He began his career in the Army where he rose to the rank of Lt. Colonel. It was in his tenure in the Army that he claimed his biggest accomplishment - An Olympic Silver Medal in the Men's Double Trap event in 2004. Having retired prematurely from the Army in 2013, he ventured into politics by contesting the 2014 Lok Sabha elections from the BJP. He is the serving Minister Of State For Information and Broadcasting. We at SRCC are proud to have Shri. Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore at the Youth Conference 2016.
